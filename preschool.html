PRE-SCHOOL DESIGN

Design Concept

The design of this preschool is inspired by elements of nature with the intention of creating a relaxing and inviting atmosphere for children to explore. The interior is shaped like a tree, with the trunk splitting into separate parts producing different rooms of discovery.The central connection for this entire design is the geometric climbing cubes encouraging the children to take steps toward new areas of realization. With the tree representing the passage of time, its overall message is to encourage the children to learn, grow and develop.
